The Space

A historic loft on Division

Built in the 1920s as a neighborhood commercial building, 1600 SE Division was reimagined in 2013–14 by Emerick Architects into an award-winning warehouse-style loft. The adaptive reuse kept the industrial bones while adding modern, net-zero design — think factory-style windows, exposed structure, and a wraparound roof terrace overlooking the city.
