Original Artwork by Gary Hirsch

“How to Make Friends” — signed 3' × 2' acrylic on board

This extraordinary donation is a signed, original 3ft × 2ft acrylic on board painting titled "How to Make Friends" by Gary Hirsch — a one-of-a-kind work of art generously contributed by a family in our community.

Gary Hirsch is a celebrated Portland-based artist and storyteller known for his joyful, whimsical, and deeply human work — his paintings are instantly recognizable for their warmth, color, and ability to make you feel something the moment you look at them. "How to Make Friends" is a title that could not feel more at home in an auction for our elementary school, making this piece as meaningful as it is beautiful.

Original signed works by Gary rarely come up for auction, making this an exceptional and truly special opportunity for any art lover or collector to own something they will cherish for a lifetime.
